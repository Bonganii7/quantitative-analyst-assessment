# Question 4 Two-Year Sales Forecast

## Baseline (Latest Historical Period)

- Latest Active Customers: 175
- Average Daily Food per Customer: 316.62 portions
- Model R^2: 0.9991

## Forecast Methodology

- Linear regression fitted to cumulative monthly active customers
- Forecast: 24 months
- Scenario bands: slope +/- 1.96 x standard error
- Monthly food demand = Customers x Avg Daily Food x Days in Month

## Two-Year Projection, The BAse

- Year 1 Forecast Total: 23,967,963 portions
- Year 2 Forecast Total: 30,782,646 portions

## Internal Factors

- Historical customer acquisition trend
- Current insect ownership distribution
- Average food demand per customer
- Stability of biological feeding requirements

## External Factors Not modelled(Limitations) 

- Inflation and feed cost pressures
- Economic conditions affecting hobby spending
- Supplier reliability and logistics
- Regulatory considerations for exotic pets

## Key Assumptions

- Linear customer growth continues over forecast horizon
- No churn modelled
- Average daily food per customer remains stable
- No structural shift in insect ownership behaviour
